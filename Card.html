Cat


The cat is a small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Weight: adult male 4 kg, female 3kg
  • Sleeping hours: 13 to 14 hours a day.
  • Lifespan: 12 to 15 years.

Cats have powerful night vision, allowing them to see at light levels six times lower than what a human needs in order to see.
